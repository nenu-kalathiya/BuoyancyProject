Our Products

For exhaustive range of organic/in-organic fertilizers, we’re the right choice

Buoyancy® exported fertilizers enhance the growth of cultivation. With our exhaustive range of organic and in-organic fertilizers our clients are benefited by enhancing the quality and effectiveness of soil by either adding nutrients or by changing the water retention and aeration required. Our Supplied fertilizers are manufactured using highest quality equipment, raw material and most modern technologies. The company continues to out-shine and position itself stronger year by year in the fertilizer business. Buoyancy offers customized packaging services for all fertilizers exported, ranging from 25KG to 1200 Kg bags to cater different requirements as per geographies of our clients.

The most usual packing is done using PE bags and readily available. Our customized packaging services have received a lot of appreciation as it reduces the importer’s costs of unloading as well as they are able to use these fertilizers as per required quantities without any wastage.

We also provide customized labeling by which our clients can develop their own brand in their markets without having any extra costs. Please contact us for more information on this. For your assurances and peace of mind, we are engaged with SGS, Bureau Veritas & Inspectorate and we take their services of inspection for quality and quantity at loading port and even at discharge port loading port and even at discharge port(if required) and a certificate is issued by default for every cargo that is exported from the company.
