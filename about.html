Company Information

BUOYANCY IMPEX PRIVATE LIMITED is an independent, privately held global enterprise on trading and Exports of Petroleum derivatives like Bitumen, Base oils, Sulphur & Polymers. And Fertilizers. Established in year 2005, today it is one of the leading petroleum trading company with prominence and stability in International market. The company is recognized for its world-class transparent services, experience, reliability & quick delivery time with its operations spread across several continents. The company’s core management and technical team are built of highly experienced professionals, with extensive exposure in procurement, material handling, logistics & operations and documentation.
